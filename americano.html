Americano

The Americano is a classic coffee beverage that originated in Italy and gained popularity in the United States. It is a simple yet delicious drink that consists of hot water poured over a shot or two of espresso. The resulting drink is smooth, bold, and often enjoyed black or with a touch of sugar. The Americano offers a balanced flavor profile, with the richness of espresso and the added volume of hot water. It is a popular choice for those who appreciate the strong taste of espresso but prefer a milder and more diluted version. Whether sipped leisurely at a coffee shop or enjoyed on-the-go, the Americano is a timeless and refreshing coffee option.
