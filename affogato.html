Affogato

Affogato is a delightful Italian dessert that combines the rich flavors of espresso and gelato. The word "affogato" translates to "drowned" in Italian, referring to the act of pouring hot espresso over a scoop of cold gelato. This simple yet indulgent dessert brings together the contrasting temperatures and textures of the two elements, resulting in a harmonious combination of bitter and sweet flavors. The hot espresso melts the gelato slightly, creating a luscious blend of creamy and velvety goodness. Affogato is a popular choice for coffee and dessert lovers, offering a satisfying treat that stimulates the senses with every spoonful.
